About GrowAgri

GrowAgri is a project aimed at assisting farmers in predicting the most suitable crops for their agricultural conditions. By providing data on nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, temperature, humidity, pH value, and rainfall, GrowAgri offers tailored crop recommendations.

Our mission is to improve agricultural productivity and sustainability by leveraging data-driven insights to optimize crop selection.
